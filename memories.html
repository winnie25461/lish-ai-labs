LOVE AND MEMORIES.

Love and memories'
are everywhere
like spring's leafing trees
in enjoyment to share

For summer is coming
in the full grace
and assome blooming
for many ways
the hour is here 
for a new fresh dreams
with its growth everywhere
and river streams
for summer
is giving days of beauty
and we are living 
in its care free
remembering day old
when dark was in windows
winter frosty cold
and light in horizon glows
all the time of night
and dreams of faraway
glistening starry flight
in all their faraway play.
